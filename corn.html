Corn

Corn is one of the most important crops in the Philippines. It serves as a staple food, a key ingredient for animal feed, and a major source of income for Filipino farmers. Corn plays a significant role in ensuring food security and supporting the agricultural economy.

  • 🌽 High quality corn seeds
  • ☀️ Suitable for dry and rainy season planting
  • 📦 Available in certified and hybrid varieties

Available Corn Varieties

Yellow Corn

Widely used for animal feed and high in energy content.

White Corn

Commonly consumed as food and suitable for various local dishes.

Hybrid Corn

High yielding variety with improved resistance to pests and diseases.
