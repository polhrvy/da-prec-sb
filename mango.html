Mango Seedlings

Mango is one of the most popular and profitable fruit crops in the Philippines. Planting high-quality mango seedlings ensures healthy tree growth, early fruiting, and high-quality harvests. Mango farming provides sustainable income opportunities and supports both local and export markets.

  • 🌱 Healthy and grafted seedlings
  • ☀️ Suitable for tropical and dry climates
  • 📦 Available in premium and export-quality varieties

Available Mango Varieties

Carabao (Philippine Mango)

Sweet and aromatic variety known for export-quality fruits.

Pico

Smaller fruit with a balanced sweet and tangy flavor.

Katchamita

Green mango variety ideal for fresh consumption and pickling.
