About Us

The Department of Agriculture – Pangasinan Research and Experiment Center (DA-PREC) is a government institution dedicated to advancing agricultural research, innovation, and extension services in Pangasinan and the Ilocos Region.

Formerly known as ILIARC Satellite Station III, DA-PREC was established as part of the Ilocos Integrated Agricultural Research Center (ILIARC) to conduct on-farm research and field testing of modern farming technologies. These technologies are carefully evaluated under real farm conditions to ensure they are practical, cost-effective, and beneficial to farmers.

Located in Brgy. Tebag East, Sta. Barbara, Pangasinan, DA-PREC serves one of the country’s most important agricultural areas. Pangasinan is a major producer of rice, corn, mango, vegetables, fisheries, and livestock, and more than half of its people depend on farming for their livelihood. Sta. Barbara itself is known for its rich farmlands and long-established mango seedling nurseries.

DA-PREC supports farmers by developing and promoting climate-smart, environment-friendly, and productivity-enhancing technologies. It also introduces modern tools such as Precision Rice Farming, Geo-referencing, Rice Crop Manager, and the Philippine Rice Information System (PRiSM) to help farmers make better decisions, reduce costs, and increase yields.

Through strong partnerships with farmers, local government units, and agricultural stakeholders, DA-PREC continues to serve as a center of research, innovation, and technology transfer working toward a productive, profitable, sustainable, and competitive agricultural sector in Pangasinan.
